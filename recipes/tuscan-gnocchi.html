Tuscan Gnocchi

Description

Here is yet another riveting recipe from the depths of my weird brain. Again, i'm just going to get straight to the point, no dilly-dallying!

Ingredients

  • One pound Gnocchi
  • Olive oil
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • Minced garlic to taste
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth or stock
  • 1 and 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup of your favorite pasta sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oil in medium sized pan or Wok
  2. Add onion and garlic, and saute until onion is translucent
  3. Add gnocchi to pan, and saute for 2 to 3 minutes
  4. Add chicken broth, heavy whipping cream, pasta sauce, and salt and pepper, then stir
  5. Cook covered for 5 minutes
  6. Remove cover and stir through spinach. Cook until wilted
  7. Turn off heat and stir through parmesan cheese
  8. Serve warm
