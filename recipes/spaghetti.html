Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Angel hair spaghetti noodles
  • Newman's Own "Sockarooni" pasta sauce
  • One pound ground beef
  • One half large onion, diced
  • Minced garlic to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Cracked pepper to taste
  • Oregano to taste

Steps

  1. Bring salted water to a boil. Add a splash of olive oil to avoid pasta sticking. add pasta once boiling and cook according to instructions on box
  2. Dice onions and garlic, and saute in olive oil until onion is translucent
  3. Add ground beef to pan and season with salt, pepper, and oregano. Saute until browned
  4. Once meat is browned, add pasta sauce and cook until heated through
  5. Drain pasta once finished, and serve with meat sauce
