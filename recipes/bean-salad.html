Bean Salad

Description

Hello for a third time! So, Bean Salad may not sound super exciting, and in some ways, you're absolutely correct! However, it is super delicious and easy to make. Also, it makes a great side for the cookout, pot-luck, or (if you add chicken like in this specific recipe) a quick weeknight dinner option. So, I don't know, here you go, I guess!

Ingredients

  • Your 2 or 3 favorite beans! I prefer Chick peas and Black beans, But really and bean will work!
  • Cooked chicken cubed and seasoned to your liking
  • 1 cup fresh Spinach, chopped
  • Onion powder to taste
  • Garlic powder to taste
  • Himalayan pink salt and cracked pepper to taste
  • Your favorite italian salad dressing to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients, and mix well
  2. Serve immediately, or refridgerate for 24 hours to let flavors emulsify
